PRESENTATION

We have done our best to display the colors of the products as accurately as possible. But it may be that partly due to the settings of your screen, they will deviate slightly from the actual product.

Leather is a natural product, a material that breathes and is flexible. The visible veins, pores, scars, insect bites and light strength and color differences in the skin characterize the authenticity of your product. These are not material errors and do not constitute grounds for discounts or refunds. It is important to follow the following maintenance instructions, so that you can enjoy your product for a long time to come.

  • Never keep in plastic or store for a long time.
  • It can be discolored by direct sunlight or heavy rain.
  • If the product has gotten wet, dry at room temperature, not in the sun or stove or heating.
  • Treat dried raindrops and other light spots with a fine soft brush or leather sponge with a dry foam of leather soap. These spots also often disappear by rubbing two surfaces of the product's leather together.
  • After getting wet and removing the dried raindrops, spray the product again with a water-repellent colorless leather spray.
  • Never treat stains with corrosive solvents.
  • Stiff leather becomes supple again by treating it with leather spray.
  • Suede can stain in the beginning.
